Lizbeth Lopez

Fashion Designer

Women's fashion designer Lizbeth Lopez has been at the forefront of the fashion industry for over 5 years now.

In an effort to expand the influence of her brand, she plans on collaborating with other high end designers to gain more popularity.

Lopez dedicates four hours of her day reading through criticism so she knows what she can improve her brand on and make it one that everyone will love.
